The Synergy Cube

A 540-card unpowered cube built on archetypes and synergies — where every card is chosen to support a theme, not just raw power.

About

What is the Synergy Cube?

The Synergy Cube started as a max-power Legacy cube and evolved into something more interesting: an environment where the power level is deliberately flatter so synergistic, archetype-driven decks can actually compete. Skullclamps and Oppositions were swapped out for cards that reward drafters who find the thread running through their seat.

The mono-colored sections each contain multiple distinct themes that overlap and play well together — so you're rarely forced on rails, but always rewarded for reading the signals. It drafts best at six players, though we make it work whenever we can scrounge up the headcount.

The Cube Cobra site has everything on there for my cube, so there will not be a necessary push to build out a cube section on this site here unfortunately (or fortunately); please explore!
