About

More Information

I love to play games with emergent gameplay. Anything that allows the player the agency to play how they want.

This blog is a general gaming blog for when I want to write things about games, design, etc. Its main use is for blogging cube draft decks.

I have several cubes on Cube Cobra. Many of these cubes are "set cubes", but my pride and joy is my synergy unpowered cube. Please give it a look!

I also play MTG Commander with a pretty steady play group. I've started tracking metrics — check out the Commander Stats.
